Our Story

Founded in 2021, Apna Payment Services Private Limited was born out of a simple frustration - the long queues at toll plazas and the hassle of getting genuine FASTags from reliable sources. Our founders, having experienced this pain firsthand during their frequent travels, decided to create a solution.

What started as a small initiative to help friends and family get authentic FASTags quickly has now grown into India's most trusted FASTag platform, serving over 1 million customers across 50+ cities through Apna Payment Services.

We partner directly with major banks like Kotak, SBI, Bajaj, and IDFC to ensure you get genuine FASTags with the fastest activation and best customer support in the industry.
